Two examples runs of NuSD. Prospect is selected as an example detector. 
To setup the detector, prospect.mac, which is located under /macros, 
is used.

inverseBetaDecay.txt >>> In this example run, a total of 100 inverse beta 
decay events are simulated.

generic.txt          >>> In this example run, a total of 100 electron energy 
deposition events are simulated.
